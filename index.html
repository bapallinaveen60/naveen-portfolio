About me

Earth observation meets
machine intelligence

I am a geospatial data scientist specialising in GeoAI, remote sensing analytics, and spatiotemporal modelling. My work sits at the intersection of satellite Earth observation and modern AI — using data from GPM, INSAT, and ERA5 to build models that matter for climate and public health.

At IIT Hyderabad, my M.Tech thesis focuses on AI-based precipitation classification using multi-source satellite radar data. I am also a Teaching Assistant for Data Modelling in Atmospheric Science.

Before IIT Hyderabad, I worked as a Geospatial Engineer at LTIMindtree (Chennai), processing high-resolution drone datasets, building automated terrain analysis pipelines, and delivering spatial data products for infrastructure and environmental projects.

I am actively looking for full-time roles and internships in GeoAI, Earth observation, and climate data science.
